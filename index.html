Arknights

Arknights is a franchise created by companies: Hypergryph, YoStar Limited, Studio Montagne and others. In its original form, it was announced as a mobile game in the tower defense genre, after some time it acquired official mangas, anime adaptations and a huge range of personal music. Development is also underway on “Arknights: EndField”, a possible sequel to Arknights.
